Backstory

Changelings were once a fabled race that once lived among humans in disguise. Changeling mothers would slip into the homes of mortals at night and switch their children with one of their own to trick humans into raising their offspring as if they were their own. Ezekiel's mother did the same with her own child, but during this time was a harsh period in the race's life. Humans had long since found out about the existence of Changeling and had already hunted the creatures to near extinction. However, despite his mother's best efforts to hide her son amidst the humans at the age of eighteen Ezekiel's true form was revealed in an accident when Ezekiel slaughtered a home invader to protect his human parents.
Horrified by the being that had lived right under their very noses Ezekiel's 'parents' turned him in to an underground scientific society known simply as 'The Alchemists'. These scientists forced the Changeling to go through numerous experiment meant to test the boy's abilities to shape-shift. Once satisfied they went onto to graft the genetic code of hundreds of animals into his own DNA with no thought to the negative repercussions this could have for the poor changeling.
After years of these experiments the Alchemists science had ultimately led to Ezekiel's freedom. The changeling was no longer recognizable as a creature of his own race and had gained the ability to grow various limbs and characteristics of other creatures. His freedom came in the form of a monstrous insect-like pincer that had painfully grown from his back. Ezekiel used this new limb to kill several of his tormentors and escape the clutches of the mad scientists.
Upon his freedom Ezekiel found a letter float out of the sky. An invitation to a place called Ulreich University?

The appendage is covered in a thick exoskeleton as tough as aluminum while the pincers themselves are incredibly sharp and can puncture iron with ease as well as having the strength to support Ezekiel's body weight.     Along the vertebra of his spine are several three inches long spines that are as sharp as Iron, but damage taken to these can also cause damage to Ezekiel's spine.

    At the base of Ezekiel's Sternum to his navel is a second mouth filled with sharp teeth. This mouth opens directly to his stomach and eating through this mouth helps him regenerate stamina at a faster rate from food he eats, but this is also a weakness that can be exploited since the mouth opens to one of his vital organs.

    BioLich Chimera:
    Thanks to the increasing instability of his genetic matter due to his continued use of Druidism and his already unstable genetic make-up Ezekiel's body has undergone a metamorphosis to better stabilize his form and draw strength from it. However, the change itself is drastic in nature and his new body requires excessive bio-energy to sustain itself. Strength and Speed now 3x peak human levels.

    Biofeast:
    While Ezekiel can still eat regular food to sustain himself it now leaves him feeling unsatisfied and increases his hunger. Ezekiel now draws energy from the flesh of living and undead creatures allowing him to store more energy than other creatures via his ‘diet’. Ezekiel must feed once a month at least or suffer from diminished sanity and fatigue from intense hunger.

    Ezekiel's second mouth has expanded and now extends up his body into the mouth on his face. This allows him to better devour prey, but now completely seals itself until it's in use. The same as before using this mouth in combat is extremely dangerous as it opens his vitals up for attacks.

If an opponent can grab and pull out one of his spines it can cause horrendous damage or even the destruction of his entire spine.

    Vicious Cycle:
    By combining Druidism power of heightened natural regeneration and Necromancy Ezekiel has found a way to increase the rate at which his body can regenerate from damage. By first growing dead flesh in place of damaged tissue and then quickly rejuvenating it even the most painful of injuries can be mended without much strain. However, using this process to regenerate or heal damaged organs takes longer depending on the complexity of the organ and the severity of the injury. However, this will not save him from lethal injuries involving damage to the brain.
    → Ezekiel may coat his body in his combined archmage mana type and leave the excess energy dormant in his body for up to a couple of hours.
    → He may coat his limbs, as well as his chest/upper torso, but not his head. Coating larger parts of the body takes longer times to cast.
    → Coating his hands and feet ONLY can be done in a single post per limb. Meaning he can coat both his hands or both his feet in one post.
    → Coating his hands up to his elbows, or his feet up to his knees will take two posts. Meaning that charging the spell for a post and activating it on the second will allow him to coat this mana up to both his elbows, or both his knees. He may also charge it on his upper body, and activate it on your second post.
    → Coating his ENTIRE arms or ENTIRE legs will take three posts. He must decide where he plan this energy to go on his first post of charging, meaning he can’t wait until three posts in to decide what he want to coat.
    → Coating a body part in his mana will cause it to tint to his mana color slightly. This isn’t enough info for someone to know what the spell does without metagaming, but does provide a hint for perceptive opponents who watch him use the spell a few times.
    → While a body part is coated in mana, it will continue to act as normal. The spell will remain for a few hours (meaning that it cannot be kept for long periods of time) however there is no actual post limit for its duration.
    → If a body part is extremely damaged or destroyed while coated in this magic, he may use his druidic nature to begin rapidly regenerating that body part, while incorporating his own (or conjured) dead flesh to fix it. Essentially he may regenerate your body part without fear of mortal wounds or death. Regenerating a body part takes just as long as it did to coat it (so hands can be done in a post, whereas full arms would take 3).
    → Regenerating the torso works for organ damage and flesh wounds ONLY. If the torso is ripped to shreds, you’re dead. If your heart is destroyed, you’re dead. If his head is separated from his body, or his brain is destroyed Ezeki. Is. DEAD.
    → Regenerating a limb removes the excess mana from it, and must be re-cast to occur again. As well as this, there is a 1 post cooldown between losing a limb and using the ability again at all. If he regenerates his right arm, he cannot lose and regenerate his left arm in the same post, even if it is coated.
    → Any hostile magic / energy which has anti-regeneration properties (such as corruption, or spells with this sort of thing in general) will cause this spell to cease working.
    → Also understand that this needs to be completed prior to the body part being damaged. If your limb is attacked while charging the spell, it will not interrupt assuming it’s only minor damage, but extreme damage or limb loss before the ability has finished charging will cause it to stop working. That being said, if you’ve charged up for 1 post, and get hit in the hand on post 2, you may regenerate your hand, although the charging to your upper arm will cease.
    → This regeneration spell cannot be completed while in other forms, and only works on Ezekiel's natural body.
